The path of our company

From a simple idea to a growing and ambitious project.

Our journey began here

Our journey began back in 2019 with a simple, yet powerful idea — to create something that truly matters. At that time, we were just a tight-knit group of enthusiasts with big ambitions and absolutely no guarantees of success.

The Early Days

Limited resources and long nights of coding became our daily routine. Every bug was a lesson.

2021 Milestone

We launched our first functional prototype and gained the trust of our initial users.

As we moved into 2024 and beyond, we scaled our technology and expanded the team. We integrated modern web stacks and began experimenting with IoT and home automation systems.

Today, in 2026, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible, hosting our services on dedicated infrastructure and focusing on user-centric design.

This is not just a company history — it’s a living story that we write every day.
