The path of our company

From a simple idea to a growing and ambitious company.

Our journey began here

Our journey began in 2019 with a simple idea — to create something meaningful and useful. At that time, we were just a small team with big ambitions and no guarantees of success.

In the early days, we faced many challenges. Limited resources, long nights, and constant problem-solving became part of our daily routine. However, every obstacle helped us grow stronger and smarter.

By 2021, we launched our first working product and gained our first users. Their feedback became the foundation for our improvement and future development.

Over the next years, we expanded our team, improved our technology, and entered new markets. Each step forward was driven by passion and a clear vision of what we wanted to achieve.

Today, we continue to grow, innovate, and push boundaries. But no matter how far we go, we never forget where we started.

This is not the end of our story — it’s just the beginning.
